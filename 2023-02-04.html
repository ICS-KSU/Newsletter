General Announcements

Keep an eye out for these recurring events:

Help Labs: Office hours and help labs are available in many computational core classes. If you're looking for some extra support from your CC instructor, remember to check your class syllabus for times you might be able to meet up!

Tutoring: Holtz Hall is offering tutoring remotely or face-to-face! They offer tutoring for a lot of classes that are part of ICS, like MATH 100 and CC 110, 210, 310 and 315. Visit their website to see the classes that they offer tutoring for and how to schedule an appointment! You can also make appointments through the Navigate student app.

Tea Time: for fun conversations about computing (or maybe just an excuse to drink tea and eat cookies), ICS students are invited to the Computer Science Tea Time, run by Russ Feldhausen. Tea time is both online and in-person, at https://bit.ly/KSUTeaTime and in DUE 2168. You can join in on Tuesdays at 3:30PM and on Fridays at 11:30AM.
