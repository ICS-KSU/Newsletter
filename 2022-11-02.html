Opportunities

HACK K-STATE at the Hack K-State hackaton! This is a great opportunity to sharpen your coding skills, and meet some like-minded people. Even if you don't compete, you can check out the projects and talk to the competitors. Good times, cool prizes, and more. Registration is now open at hackkstate.tech

Earn a scholarship with Inoxoft. Inoxoft is offering $1000 in scholarship support for IT students interested in using that money to support self-study and the pursuit of educational opportunities. If you've got a cool idea for what you'd do, consider applying for the scholarship. Here's the link

Learn about the history of games in HIST 301B (speaking of enrollment...). Here's the course description:

This class is about history in games. The goal is to think more carefully about the meanings of the games we play and about what these games mean for our historical consciousness today. Students will investigate selected games in depth, and will also work toward designing their own historical games. For questions about this class contact Professor Brandom, ebrandom@ksu.edu.
