ICS NEWSLETTER

New This Week

If you're a KSU student who's been here one semester or more, you're eligible to apply for a continuing student scholarship through the K-State Scholarship Network. A scholarship can provide financial support for you as you work on your college degree. BUT! The deadline to set up a profile that you can use to apply is March 15th, just about a week away. So if you're interested in a scholarship, remember to set up that profile!

Opportunities

Get help with K-State Scholarship Network applications this Monday, (3/7) at 5:30-6:30 PM in Fielder 1092. You can get help writing a financial need statement, and learn tips and tricks for successfully filling out the K-State Scholarship Network form.

Learn how to get internships in Kansas City with Paul Karr, the director of engineering programs at K-State Olathe, this Thursday (3/10) and Friday (3/11). He'll help you find housing, find mentorship, and learn how to network and build industry connections. The event will be held in the Engineering Atrium from 11-2 PM on both days.

Exploring Entrepreneurship Scholarships: Are you interested in learning about how innovation and the entrepreneurial mindset will help you in your career? Applications for Exploring Entrepreneurship scholarships for fall 2022 are now available. These $1,000 scholarships are awarded to students to take their first entrepreneurship class. Priority review of applications begins in four days, on March 11.

General Annoucements

Keep an eye out for these recurring events:

Help Lab: Get in-person help from a Computer Science instructor every Wednesday from 3:30-4:45 pm in Calvin Hall room 306.

Tea Time: Have fun and hang out virtually with people in the CS department! Tea Time is a chance to catch up with what's going on in CS, meet alumni, and get introduced to KSU's industry partners. The hours are Tuesdays at 3:30 in DUE 2168, and Fridays at 10:30am on Zoom .

Tutoring: Holtz Hall is offering tutoring remotely or face-to-face! They offer tutoring for a lot of classes that are part of ICS, like MATH 100 and CC 110, 210, 310 and 315. Visit their website to see the classes that they offer tutoring for and how to schedule an appointment! You can also make appointments through the Navigate student app.
